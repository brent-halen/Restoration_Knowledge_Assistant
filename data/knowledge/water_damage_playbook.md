# Water Damage Emergency Playbook

## When this is urgent

Treat active water intrusion, standing water, sewage backup, or water near energized electrical systems as high urgency. A quick response matters because porous materials and trapped moisture can escalate damage quickly.

## First actions

1. Stop the source if it can be done safely.
2. Keep people away from contaminated or electrically unsafe areas.
3. Move contents to a dry area when practical.
4. Start documenting conditions with photos and timestamps.

## Category notes

- Category 1: clean water from a supply line or similar source.
- Category 2: gray water with some contamination potential.
- Category 3: black water or sewage; use stronger PPE and containment.

## Documentation

Record affected rooms, visible moisture, standing water depth, material types, and any safety hazards. Insurance-related work benefits from before, during, and after photos plus equipment logs.
