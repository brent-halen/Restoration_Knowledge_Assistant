# Fire And Smoke Cleanup Overview

## When this is urgent

Recent fires, active structural instability, lingering electrical hazards, or heavy smoke residue affecting occupied areas should be treated as urgent.

## First actions

1. Confirm emergency responders have released the site.
2. Prevent re-entry into unsafe areas.
3. Document soot patterns, odor intensity, and affected contents.
4. Separate salvageable contents from obvious debris when safe.

## Notes

Different soot types respond differently to dry cleaning, wet cleaning, or specialty deodorization. The assistant should avoid giving false certainty about exact treatment methods without retrieved context.
